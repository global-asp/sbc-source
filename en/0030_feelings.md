# Feelings

##
My heart feels a lot of
things.

##
I feel happy when my
granny tells us stories
in the evening.

##
I feel silly when I play
with my friend.

##
I feel bad when my dad
says he does not have
money.

##
I feel loved when my
mom gives me a hug.
